Covid-19 and It's Outbreak

What is covid-19 and how did the outbreak start?

Covid-19 is also named as corona virus. These are family of viruses that cause disease in mammals. The new corona virus has spread to nearly every country in the world since it first emerged in China. More than 1.5 million people are known to be infected and more than 89,000 deaths have been recorded.
