Covid-19 Prevention

There is currently no vaccine to prevent Covid-19
You can protect yourself and help prevent spreading to others

Do's:

  • Wash your hands frequently for every hour atleast for 20seconds
  • Cover your nose and mouth with a disposable tissue or flexed elbow when you cough or sneeze
  • Avoid close contact with people who are unwell
  • Stay home and self isolate from others in households if you are unwell
  • Increase your immunity power by taking food that are rich in vitamin (Mainly Vitamin-C) and protein

Don'ts:

  • Touch your:
    1. Eyes
    2. Nose
    3. Mouth
  • Go to crowdy places
