Symptoms of Covid-19

People may be sick with the virus for 1 to 14 days before developing the symptoms
The most common symptoms are:

  1. Fever
  2. Fatigue
  3. Dry cough

Most people recover from this disease without needing special treatment. Moreover, rarely the disease can be serious and fatal.
Older people and people with other medical conditions such as:

  • Asthma
  • diabetes
  • heart disease
May be more vulnerable to becoming severely ill.
