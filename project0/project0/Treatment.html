Covid-19 Treatments

There is no particular medicine to treat this disease. People just need to take proper care.

  • Self care

    • If you have mild symptoms, stay at home until you've recovered you can relieve your symptoms if you:

    • Rest and sleep
    • Keep warm
    • Drink plenty of liquids
    • Use room humidifier or take a hot shower

  • Medical treatments:

    • If you develop a fever, cough and have difficulty breathing, promptly seek medical care call in advance and tell your health provider of any recent travel or recent contact with travellers.
